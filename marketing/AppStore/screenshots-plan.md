# Screenshots Plan

Apple requires screenshots for 6.7", 6.1", and iPad 13" device classes. Same 8 captures across all three. Captions overlaid on each, not part of the raw screenshot.

## Capture list

1. **Home tab, populated**
   Show: balance hero ("You're owed $84"), budget summary card with one over-budget category, recent expenses with the paperclip icon on a scanned one.
   Caption: "See where you stand at a glance."

2. **Add Expense, equal split with 4 members**
   Show: amount $142.50, Groceries category, four member rows with checkboxes, split type segmented control on Equal.
   Caption: "Five split types. Equal, percent, amount, shares, or just for you."

3. **Receipt scan review with AI cleanup badges**
   Show: receipt thumbnail at top, recognition banner ("Recognized 5 items, 2 new to review"), line items with green and orange dots and the "AI" pill on a renamed item.
   Caption: "Scan a receipt. Splitway learns which items go to whom."

4. **Assignment sheet with the three "remember" options**
   Show: an item like "Whole milk gallon", member checkboxes, "Always shared / Just this time / Always Hamza's" picker.
   Caption: "Tell it once. It remembers."

5. **Settle Up screen**
   Show: "Just 2 payments to settle everyone up", two payment cards with from-to avatars, Mark paid and Open Zelle buttons.
   Caption: "Greedy debt simplification. Fewer payments to actually move."

6. **Reports tab in Just-me mode with 6-month trend**
   Show: scope toggle on "Just me", hero ("You spent $612"), donut by category, 6-month bars.
   Caption: "Your spending, household spending. Switch with one tap."

7. **Budgets screen with an over-budget category**
   Show: hero monthly card, three category cards (one under, one fully spent, one over with coral border).
   Caption: "Per-category budgets. Alerts when you cross 80 and 100 percent."

8. **Assistant tab with chips and one Q&A exchange**
   Show: capybara, the message "You're $84 ahead this month, thanks to one big grocery trip you covered for everyone," and the chip palette underneath.
   Caption: "Ask a question, get a clear answer. No free-typing required."
