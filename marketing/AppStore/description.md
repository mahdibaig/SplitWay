## Description (4,000 characters max)

```
Splitway is the household expense tracker built for adults who actually live together. Couples, families, and roommates who share rent, groceries, and the weekly Costco run. No ads, no analytics, no banking integration. Just clear records and an honest split.

WHY SPLITWAY

Existing apps treat every receipt as one line. Real life isn't like that. A Costco trip is mostly shared, with a few personal items. A power bill is split between two couples, not four individuals. Splitway is built for the way households actually spend.

SMART RECEIPT SCANNING

Snap a photo of a receipt. Splitway uses on-device Apple Vision to pull out every line item, then learns over time. After a few scans, the app remembers that milk is always shared and face wash is always Hamza's. New items get an orange dot, known items get green. One tap per item to confirm.

COUPLES AND FAMILIES, FIRST CLASS

Splitway treats groups as a real entity, not a tag. Two couples sharing rent? Split it group versus group with one tap, and let each couple settle internally. Tap a group to drill into individuals when you need to. No other app does this well.

ALL FIVE SPLIT TYPES

Equal, percentages, exact amounts, shares, or excluded. Mix and match per expense. Long-press a line on a receipt to assign quantity per person (two beers for Alice, one for Bob).

THE FRIENDLY ASSISTANT

An optional AI assistant (off by default) answers questions like "How much did I spend on groceries this month?" or "Are we over budget anywhere?" by reading a snapshot of your household. Tap a question, get an answer. No free-form chat, no hallucinated balances. Powered by DeepSeek. You can turn it off any time.

BUDGETS, BILLS, AND REPORTS

Set monthly budgets per category with alerts at 80 and 100 percent. Schedule recurring bills with automatic logging (or a prompt for variable amounts). See spending broken down by category, by person, by 3, 6, 9, or 12 month windows.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

Every expense, photo, balance, and member lives on your iPhone. iCloud sync uses your Apple ID; we never see your data. The AI assistant is the only feature that talks to a service, and only when you opt in. No ads. No analytics. No tracking. No banking integration. Ever.

NO DAILY EXPENSE CAP

Free users get unlimited expense logging. Always. That's not a Pro feature, and it never will be.

PRICING

Try Splitway free for 14 days, then pick what fits:
- Individual: $24.99 per year
- Family (up to 5 members): $39.99 per year
- Household Lifetime: $89, one time

Move from Splitwise? Built-in CSV import brings your history with you.

REQUIREMENTS

iPhone with iOS 17 or later. iCloud account recommended for sync across your devices and with household members. Apple Vision OCR works fully on device. AI assistant requires opt-in.
```
