## 1.0 (first release)

```
Welcome to Splitway. The household expense tracker built for couples, families, and roommates who share real life and real money.

What's in 1.0:
- All five split types: equal, percent, amount, shares, excluded
- Smart receipt scanning with on-device OCR and item-level learning
- Couples and families as first-class groups
- Monthly budgets per category with 80 and 100 percent alerts
- Recurring bills (fixed auto-logs, variable prompts you for the amount)
- Local notifications for budget thresholds and bills due
- Reports tab with donut, 3 to 12 month trend, and a "just me" personal view
- Optional AI assistant powered by DeepSeek (off by default, tap a chip to ask)
- Privacy-first: every record stays on your iPhone, iCloud sync uses your Apple ID, no analytics, no ads

Try Splitway free for 14 days. Then $24.99 per year, $39.99 for families, or $89 lifetime.
```
